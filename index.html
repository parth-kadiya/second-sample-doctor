Approach to Treatment

Dr. Karnav believes in treating patients with empathy, using a blend of evidence-based medicine and patient education. His goal is not just to treat heart problems but to prevent them from recurring.

Interventional Procedures

  • Angioplasty: Minimally invasive procedure to restore blood flow in blocked arteries.
  • Bypass Surgery: Surgical treatment for severe coronary artery disease.
  • Pacemaker Implantation: For patients with abnormal heart rhythms (arrhythmias).

Chronic Disease Management

  • Blood Pressure Management: Holistic and medical approaches to control hypertension.
  • Cholesterol Control: Advanced lipid profiling and lifestyle guidance.
  • Heart Failure Treatment: Comprehensive care for weakened heart function.

Preventive & Diagnostic Services

  • General Cardiac Checkup: Routine screening for heart health.
  • Stress Test & ECG: To detect early signs of cardiac issues.
  • Echocardiogram (ECHO): Detailed ultrasound to assess heart function.

All treatments are tailored individually based on patient history, lifestyle, and health condition. Dr. Karnav Patel uses evidence-based protocols and modern technology to ensure the best outcomes.
